# Week 2 Reflection

Reflect 2: Can AI help us define what it means to be human, or does it reduce us to patterns?

AI can help us think about what it means to be human, but it can also reduce people to patterns. Language models only predict words and do not truly understand meaning. This shows me that humans still matter because we bring creativity and responsibility.

AI also turns people into data and probabilities. It can learn from biased sources, which can repeat stereotypes and leave out many voices. Because AI sounds clear, it is easy to trust it, even when it does not really understand.

AI can highlight what makes humans unique, but it can also make us seem simple. It pushes me to think about human responsibility when using these tools.
