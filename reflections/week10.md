
# Week 10 Reflection

Reflect 10: How does AI change our relationship to play, rules, and narrative?

AI changes how we think about play, rules, and narrative. In my game, AI is part of the story, not the system itself. I created the rules and choices, but the game shows what it would feel like if AI controlled them. This made me think about how AI could influence decisions and reduce player control. It shows that AI can shape the experience, even if it is not actually running the game.
