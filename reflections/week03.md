# Week 3 Reflection

Reflect 3: What does “authenticity” mean when identity is co-constructed with AI?

Authenticity is being your true self, flaws and all. When identity is co-constructed with AI, like editing photos, authenticity becomes harder to maintain. AI often pushes us to remove imperfections. As humans, we also want to remove our imperfections. Instead of showing who we really are, we show an ideal version of ourselves. Authenticity then becomes a choice about how much we let AI change us.
