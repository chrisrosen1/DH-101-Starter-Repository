
# Week 4 Reflection

Reflect 4: Is AI a collaborator, tool, or plagiarist in storytelling?

I see AI mostly as a tool, but sometimes as a collaborator. When I made my cartoon, I tried a few different AI programs. Some did not keep the character consistent, and the panels did not look how I imagined. I had to switch tools to get the result I wanted. That showed me that AI does not just “do the work” on its own. I still had to make decisions and adjust things.

AI can feel like a collaborator when you go back and forth with it. But I think it only becomes plagiarism if someone copies what it makes without revising or thinking about it. For me, AI helped, but I was still the one shaping the story.
