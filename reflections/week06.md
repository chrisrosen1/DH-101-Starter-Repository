

# Week 6 Reflection

Reflect 6: What do we gain/lose when machines “read” literature for us?

When machines “read” literature for us, we gain speed and clarity. Voyant quickly turned the speech into visuals like a word cloud and a trends graph. That helped me see patterns right away. ChatGPT gave me a full analysis in seconds from a very simple prompt. It organized themes and ideas without me having to think through everything on my own.

But we also lose something. Voyant shows repetition, but it doesn’t explain meaning. ChatGPT gives a strong interpretation very quickly, but it makes the process feel automatic. I didn’t have to struggle with the text or slowly work through the ideas myself. When machines do most of the work, we save time, but we lose some of the deeper thinking that comes from reading carefully on our own.
