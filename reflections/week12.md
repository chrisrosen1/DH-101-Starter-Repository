# Week 12 Reflection

Reflect 12: Is AI sustainable? What ecological trade-offs are we willing to accept?

I don’t think AI is fully sustainable right now because of how much energy it uses, especially to train large models. Seeing the comparison to cars and flights in my infographic made that impact feel more real. At the same time, it’s hard to ignore how useful AI is, so the trade-off is convenience versus environmental cost.

Even though my own use feels small, it adds up when millions of people are using AI the same way. That’s what makes this problem difficult. It depends on a lot of people making small changes, not just one person.
