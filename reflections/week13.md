
# Week 13 Reflection

Reflect 13: Do we imagine AI futures as utopian, dystopian, or something in between? What do those visions reveal about us?

I imagine AI futures as somewhere in between, not fully good or bad. AI can make life easier and help with things like mental health, but it can also make people more dependent on it. In my project, the AI therapist is helpful, but it also replaces real human connection in some ways. That shows that even today, we already rely a lot on technology because it’s easier and more convenient. I think it reveals that people want support and efficiency, but we might sacrifice real relationships without fully realizing it.
