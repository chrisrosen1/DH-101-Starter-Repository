# Week 9 Reflection

Reflect 9: What is creativity when AI can generate endlessly? Where is the human in generative work?

Creativity feels more like making certain decisions when AI can generate endlessly. It is less about producing something from nothing and more about choosing what stands out and what matters. Humans are still important because they create the system, guide the process, and decide what is worth keeping. Building a generator showed that even simple rules can produce a lot of variation. The experience felt confusing at first since it was not fully automated. I was still in control of how the process was set up and, to some extent, the output, while the AI added its own variations.
